Rhythm - Notes

Now that you know the names of the notes, we need to figure out how long they last. Each note lasts a certain number of beats and you can tell how many based on what the note looks like. There are many different types of notes but we will focus on three of the most basic here.

A quarter note lasts for 1 beat.



A half note lasts for 2 beats.



A whole note lasts for 4 beats.


On the next page, you will be shown two notes and asked to add their total beats together.
