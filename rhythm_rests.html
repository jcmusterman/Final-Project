Rhythm - Rests

A rest is a symbol for slience. For every note there is a corresponding rest.

A quarter rest lasts for 1 beat.



A half rest lasts for 2 beats.



A whole rest lasts for 4 beats.


On the next page, you will be shown two rests and asked to add their total beats together.
