Notes of the Bass Clef Staff

From bottom to top, the notes on the lines of the bass clef are G, B, D, F, and A.

You can remember this using a phrase like Good Boys Do Fine Always.

The spaces of the bass clef spell are A, C, E, G.

This one can be remembered with All Cows Eat Grass.

Again, as you go up the staff you move forward in the alphabet, looping back to A after G.
