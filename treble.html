Notes on the Treble Clef Staff

From bottom to top, the notes on the lines of the treble clef are E, G, B, D, and F.

You can remember this using a phrase like Every Good Boy Does Fine.

The spaces of the treble clef spell the word F A C E.

Notice that as you go up the staff you move forward in the alphabet, looping back to A after G.
